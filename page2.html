Characters in Noli me tangere
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Crisóstomo is a smart, young man who just came back from his 7 years of study in Europe
  • He is the love interest of Maria Clara
  • He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra and grandson of Don Saturino
Maria Clara de los Santos
  • Maria Clara stayed in Beaterio for a long time before meeting with Crisostomo Ibarra
  • She is the Daughter of Pia Alba and Kapitan Tiago
  • She is the love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra
Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso was the former kura of San Diego
  • He is the actual father of Maria Clara as a result of his affair with Pia Alba
  • He, along with Kaptian Tiago, disapproved of Maria Clara's relationship with Crisostomo Ibarra
Kapitan Tiago
  • Kapitan Tiago is the husband Pia Alba and is the father of Maria Clara
  • He is also a very rich person due to him making opium
  • He pays people to pray for him
  • Kapitan Tiago is very close to the upper-class people like Padre Damaso
Sisa's Family
  • It consists of Crispin(Youngest), Basilio(Oldest), and Sisa(The Mother)
  • Crispin and Basilio were taken to work for the church due to them being accused of stealing 32 pesos
